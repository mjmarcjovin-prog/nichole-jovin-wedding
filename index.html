FAQs
WHEN SHOULD I RSVP BY?

Please send your RSVP as soon as you can. The earlier, the better!

The latest we can accept your response is on August 25, 2026. If we will not receive a response by this date, it will be marked as a “NO”.

We will miss you celebrating with us; however, we must properly provide the total guest count to our venue and suppliers. We hope you understand.

To RSVP, go to the “Guest RSVP” tab.

WHAT SHOULD I WEAR?

Refer to the attire guide under the “Attire” tab. Check it out!

Note: STRICTLY no wearing of white dress, jeans, or slippers.

CAN I BRING A DATE (PLUS ONE)?

Unfortunately, no. We will specifically inform you if you can have a plus one. We hope you understand that we can no longer accommodate those who are not listed on our guest list due to limited seats at the venue.

ARE KIDS ALLOWED?

We certainly love your kids, but due to necessity rather than choice, we regretfully cannot accommodate them at the venue. The only kids attending are those who are part of the entourage and those requested. We hope you understand and enjoy the night off.

IS THE CEREMONY AND RECEPTION AT THE SAME PLACE?

No. The church and reception hall are not located in the same place. More details about the locations can be found in the “Venue” tab. Check it out!

IS THERE A PARKING SPACE PROVIDED?

Yes. There is free parking available for all wedding guests.

WHAT TIME SHOULD I ARRIVE?

We encourage our wedding guests to be at the venue 30 minutes before our 2:00 PM ceremony time, to have ample time to settle and find a seat.

Kindly consider traffic and travel time going to the venue.

WHAT DO I DO IF I’M LATE TO THE CEREMONY?

If you are late to the ceremony, please do not walk down the aisle and try to find a seat.

While we want all of our friends and family there, we would appreciate it if you would ask assistance from our coordinator or wait to join us until the reception. Thank you for understanding.

CAN I TAKE PHOTOS OR VIDEOS DURING THE CEREMONY?

We encourage our guests to refrain from taking photos or videos during the ceremony as we have hired an amazing team to capture the moments. Guests may still do so.

WHAT SHOULD I DO ONCE THE CEREMONY IS DONE?

There will be a cocktail hour after the ceremony wherein afternoon snacks will be served.

You may also choose this time to find your seat, mingle with other guests, use the photo booth, and get your souvenirs. Our coordinators will be available if you need any assistance.

CAN I SEAT ANYWHERE DURING THE RECEPTION?

Yes. Feel free to sit anywhere during the reception, pick a spot you like, mingle around, and make yourself at home.

Note: Our principal sponsors will have designated seats reserved for them.

WHEN IS THE APPROPRIATE TIME TO LEAVE?

It took us almost a year to plan a wedding that everyone will hopefully enjoy. We encourage our guests to finish the program.

We aim to end at 9:00 PM to 10:00 PM.

HOW CAN I HELP THE COUPLE TO HAVE THE BEST TIME ON THEIR WEDDING DAY?

Be there on time. Wear appropriate attire. Stay until the end of the program. Participate and have FUN!
